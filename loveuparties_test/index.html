Love U Princess & Pamper Parties

All about us

Hi I'm Carrie and have been working with children for the past 20 years in performing arts. So you could say I'm an expert on glitter and girls and making your child feel like a star. My ethos is U. It's all about U. I want them to feel so special, magical & confident.

I absolutely love hosting the parties. For me the look on their faces when they walk into the venue - it's priceless. And when the wows and gasps happen - it's magical.

I'm so proud of the way the venue looks, I let all the parents of the children have a sneaky peak first. They're amazed and always want an adult version, and for me that says it all.

Any party can be quite a stressful occasion for busy mums and dads! So it is our mission to provide fabulous fun-filled parties - taking the worries away from parents.

What happens at Love U Princess & Pamper Parties?

Well it's all about U. The party is tailor made for your child's specific age group. The birthday girl will be treated like a true princess and will be the first to have her pink gown on. Once each Pamper Party Guest has been fully gowned the excitement starts! Pink fizz is poured into champagne flutes. The party girl is toasted and pictures are taken under the love U V.I.P sign. Then the door is opened into the pamper palace. Their faces are magical. The Party is then officially opened with a 'cutting of the ribbon' by the party girl.

Join UDanceUk, a place and community that will instil positivity and confidence. The number one performing arts school, providing dance classes in Crawley, Burgess Hill, Crawley Down, Worthing, Ferring, Lancing, Billingshurst, Ashington and Pulborough.
